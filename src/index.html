What is LASIK?

Are you tired of wearing glasses or contacts? Regarded as one of the fastest growing and successful medical procedures, LASIK Surgery has eliminated the need for contacts and glasses for millions of people since the technique was granted FDA approval in the late 1990s.

Thomas Eye Group was one of the first to perform this procedure under FDA protocol, and our experienced physicians have performed more than 25,000 vision correction procedures. In addition, Thomas Eye Group was voted the 2015, 2016, and 2017 "Best LASIK Doctor" in Atlanta by JEZEBEL Magazine and offers Atlanta's most precise, advanced NASA-approved bladeless LASIK technology.

Blade Free LASIK Technology

Thomas Eye Group offers Custom Bladeless Laser Vision Correction treatments including iLASIK™. With Bladeless LASIK, the flap is created using a laser as opposed to a manual blade, allowing for precise depth and diameter. We use only the most advanced, NASA approved technology. 

Bladeless LASIK at Thomas Eye Group has the ability to restore vision to 20/20 or even better for people suffering from nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. This procedure provides a significant improvement in lifestyle and freedom from the hassles and irritations of lenses. Specific results depend upon your eyesight - each result is unique to the patient
